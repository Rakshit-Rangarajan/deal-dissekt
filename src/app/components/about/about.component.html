About Me

I'm a high-achieving finance professional with a passion for simplifying complex financial structures. My journey began with a strong academic foundation, where I earned the 7th All India Rank in the IPCC (CA Inter) examinations.

Since then, I have specialized in the Transaction Tax and M&A space, gaining extensive experience at top-tier firms like BSR (KPMG), Deloitte, and currently EY. My work involves navigating the intricate world of Mergers and Acquisitions, group restructurings, and tax efficiency for major PE funds and corporate entities.

Through Deal Dissekt, I aim to provide a one-stop solution to all financial curiosity—minus the jargon. I believe that understanding high-stakes finance shouldn't be limited by complex terminology, and my mission is to make these insights accessible to everyone.
